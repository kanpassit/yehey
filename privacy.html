Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 27, 2026  ·  Last Updated: April 27, 2026

KanPassIt ("we," "us," or "our") operates the KanPassIt exam prep application available at kanpassit.com. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and a hashed password via our authentication provider, Supabase.
  • Quiz Progress Data: We store your quiz answers, accuracy rates, attempt counts, and domain performance scores to power your personalized study experience.
  • API Keys: If you enter a Claude API key to enable AI analysis features, it is stored locally in your browser's localStorage and is never transmitted to our servers.
  • Usage Data: We may collect information about how you interact with the app, including pages visited and features used, to improve the service.
  • Device Information: Standard browser and device information (browser type, operating system, screen size) may be collected automatically.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the KanPassIt service
  • To sync your progress across devices when you are signed in
  • To personalize your study experience based on your performance data
  • To send important service-related communications (account verification, password resets)
  • To display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve app features

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for authentication and to maintain your session while signed in (provided by Supabase).
  • Preference Cookies: Store your settings and preferences locally (such as your consent choice and study configuration).
  • Advertising Cookies: We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our free tier. Google and its partners may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our site and other sites on the internet. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or optout.aboutads.info. You can also manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

For more information about how Google uses data from sites that use its advertising services, see: How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data independently:

  • Supabase — Authentication and database hosting. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Google AdSense — Advertising services. Google Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic Claude API — AI analysis features (only when you provide your own API key). Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • Vercel — Website hosting and content delivery. Vercel Privacy Policy

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only as follows:

  • With service providers listed above who help us operate the application
  • When required by law or to protect our rights
  • With your consent

6. Data Retention

We retain your account and progress data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. You may delete your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at support@kanpassit.com. Locally stored data (localStorage) can be cleared through your browser settings.

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including HTTPS encryption for all data in transit. Authentication is managed by Supabase using secure, salted password hashing. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

8. Children's Privacy

KanPassIt is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • The right to access the data we hold about you
  • The right to correct inaccurate data
  • The right to delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • The right to opt out of personalized advertising
  • The right to data portability

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@kanpassit.com.

10. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have the right to know what personal information we collect, to request deletion of that information, and to opt out of the "sale" of personal information. We do not sell personal information. To submit a privacy request, contact us at support@kanpassit.com.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
