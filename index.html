Exams We Cover
🏥
NCLEX-RN
National Council Licensure Examination — Registered Nurse

The NCLEX-RN is the national licensure exam for registered nurses in the United States and Canada. It tests clinical judgment, patient safety, and nursing knowledge across domains including Medical-Surgical, Pharmacology, Mental Health, Maternal Newborn, and Pediatrics. KanPassIt covers all major domains with hundreds of adaptive practice questions aligned to the NCLEX-RN test plan.

💊
NCLEX-PN
National Council Licensure Examination — Practical Nurse

The NCLEX-PN licenses practical and vocational nurses across the US. The exam covers safe and effective care, health promotion, psychosocial integrity, and physiological integrity. Our adaptive question bank helps LPN and LVN candidates focus on their weakest domains and build clinical reasoning skills before test day.

🩺
CNA Certification
Certified Nursing Assistant — State Board Exam

CNA certification exams test foundational nursing assistant skills including patient care, communication, infection control, and basic clinical procedures. KanPassIt helps CNA candidates build confidence with scenario-based practice questions that mirror real state board exam formats.

👔
SHRM-CP
SHRM Certified Professional — HR Certification

The SHRM-CP is a globally recognized HR certification from the Society for Human Resource Management. It validates competency in HR strategy, talent acquisition, employee relations, compensation, and organizational development. Our practice questions are mapped to the SHRM Body of Competency and Knowledge (BoCK), covering both behavioral and technical competencies.

🏆
SHRM-SCP
SHRM Senior Certified Professional

The SHRM-SCP is designed for senior-level HR professionals responsible for developing and executing HR strategy. It covers leadership, global HR, organizational effectiveness, and workforce planning at an advanced level. Our SHRM-SCP module includes scenario-based situational judgment questions for the exam's strategy-focused format.

🤖
AB-900
Microsoft 365 Copilot — AI Certification

The AB-900 exam certifies proficiency in Microsoft 365 Copilot, Microsoft's AI-powered productivity suite. It covers AI fundamentals in the workplace, Copilot capabilities across Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Teams, and Outlook, responsible AI use, and organizational adoption strategies.
