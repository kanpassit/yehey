Skip Button — Credit Logic
Skipped questions should NOT award credit if user later guesses correctly. This preserves honest domain scoring and prevents weak-area inflation. Decide: does a skip reset on session restart, or persist in Supabase?
Analytics
Third-party tool or Supabase-native?
Key behaviors to track: drop-off by subject, skip rates, study plan engagement, free→paid conversion. Supabase already holds progress data — may be sufficient pre-launch. Evaluate Mixpanel, PostHog, or Plausible at Phase 2.
